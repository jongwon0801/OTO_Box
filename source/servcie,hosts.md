
#### /lib/systemd/system/reversessh.service

#### (정은치엘로 B, 아이비힐, 예성유토피아)

```
[Unit]
Description=Reverse SSH Service
After=network.target

[Service]
Type=idle
ExecStart=/bin/sh /home/pi/reversesshservice.sh

[Install]
WantedBy=multi-user.target
```
